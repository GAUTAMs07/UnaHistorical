Historical Places in Una


Chintpurni Mata mandir

Chintapurni Mata Mandir is a Hindu place of worship. With loads of reverence and adoration, we went to perform puja there. Mata is here, for all of us. The temple can be reached via Una or via Dharamshala. You have to get a 'parchi' before you get an entry to the temple, and this building is approximately two kilometres away from the temple. Management should develop a better system for buying 'parchi' (tokens). It took us around one hour to get the parchi - and that too after a long wait in the queue and lots of push and shove!! Jai Maa Chintpurni

Shiva Bari

Shiv Bari, a historical temple of lord Shiva, situated in the way of Chintpurni Mandir at Gagret, said to be made by Guru Drona ,in Mahabharata time. This is a crowd less temple, ideal for worship through deep of own soul.

Solah singhi Dhar

Solah Singhi Dhar or Jaswan Dhar Range is a prominent tourist attraction in Una. The first 14 km of the hill range, starting near Talwara on the Beas river, lies in the Kangra District and ultimately culminates as a ridge separating Kangra from the Una District.
